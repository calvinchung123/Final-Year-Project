DIRT
-1 Good Rule of thumb is -1 for every 2 signs of dirt
Maximum 3 can be deducted from a pair of shoes for dirt


CREASES
-1 If there are a crease at the front of the shoe automatically -1
-.5+ For every additional crease (creases in the same branch/area) -0.5
Maximum 3 can be deducted from a pair of shoes for creases








GRADING DESCRIPTION
10 Perfect, mint condition. No signs of creases or dirt
9 Near mint signs of dirt or small creases
8 Minor imperfections. Some scratching or creases
7 Visible imperfections. Signs of creases, maybe signs of dirt
6 Used. Multiple creases and signs of dirt
5 Flawed. Multiple cases of the defects noted above
This shoe grading rubric is a deduction-based rubric. A shoe will start at 10 (Perfect) and end at 5(flawed). Following the 2 defect rubrics you can grade a shoe with a combination of the defects present on a shoe.
